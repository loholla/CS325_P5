"Apple Under Fire: Exploring the Implications of the DOJ Lawsuit on iPhone Users' Experience and Ecosystem Diversity"
Apple's consumer-friendly approach dates to its iconic Mac computer. Recently, the US sued Apple for smartphone market monopolization, accusing Apple of restricting competition. Potential changes may grant iPhone users access to more versatile apps, provide better messaging between platforms, and allow more interactivity with non-Apple devices. Critics fear the impact on user experience.
