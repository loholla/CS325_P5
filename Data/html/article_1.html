"Under Siege: The Cyberattack on Fulton County - A Symphony of Disruption and Resilience"

Fulton County, Georgia is dealing with an ongoing cyberattack causing system outages in court filings, tax processing, and more. The attack, which also impacts the District Attorney's office, is still unresolved, with no known motive. Similar attacks on other municipalities have resulted in significant financial costs and service disruptions. The FBI is involved in investigating the incident.
